MV DANCING CLUB

- 簡介 -

我們是MV舞蹈社，顧名思義，是一個專門跳MV舞蹈的社團。


我們雖然沒有好幾百人的大陣仗，但我們卻是由一群熱愛跳舞的孩子們組成的大家庭。


我們的舞風多元，從歐美的MV，到日本、台灣、韓國的MV我們都有涉獵。


希望在未來，我們能帶來更多樣、更有創意的演出。



We are MV dance club,and our members come from different departments, different downtown, with totally different personality.


Although we are not a huge club with hundreds of people,what we own in common is the fervent love for dancing.


Not only America MV, but also Japan, Taiwan, Korea MV dance are we focus on.


We hope to enhance our horizon to another level and perform more diversely. This is our goal and we will do it!
